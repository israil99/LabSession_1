In this HTML paragraphs tutorial, you’ll learn that elements need some additional help to be nice and neat on your website and how to do that. Also, you’ll learn about the poem problem. Poems sometimes are quite difficult to understand, but they’re also problematic when it comes to displaying in browsers. You’ll find out how to fix the poem problem, and many other techniques on how to present a clean text.

     There are not many things to know about writing HTML paragraphs in your documents, but you should know and follow the basic rules to make it as neat as you want it to be.
